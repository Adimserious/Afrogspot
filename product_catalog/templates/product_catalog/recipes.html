Preparation Steps:
  1. Prepare the proteins: Wash and season your meats, stockfish, and dry fish. Boil until tender.
  2. Cook the base: Add palm oil, ground crayfish, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  3. Add fresh fish: Carefully add your fresh fish to the pot and let it cook gently to absorb the flavors.
  4. Incorporate okro and ugba: Add the sliced okro and ugba. Stir lightly and allow to simmer.
  5. Finish with uziza: Add the uziza leaves and adjust seasoning with salt and cubes. Let it simmer for 3-5 minutes.
